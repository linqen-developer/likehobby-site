Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about LikeHobby.

When will LikeHobby launch?
We're planning to launch the beta version in Q4 2025, with early access for waitlist members. The full platform launch is scheduled for Q1 2026.
Is LikeHobby free to use?
Yes, LikeHobby will have a free tier with access to core features. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the main community experience will always be free.
What hobby categories will be available?
We'll launch with several popular categories including photography, cooking, gardening, arts & crafts, music, reading & writing, fitness, and technology. More categories will be added based on community interest.
How can I become a mentor on the platform?
Once we launch, users will be able to apply for mentor status within their hobby communities. Mentors will be selected based on their experience, knowledge, and positive community contributions.
Will there be a mobile app?
Yes, we plan to release mobile apps for iOS and Android in Q2 2026, following our web platform launch.
